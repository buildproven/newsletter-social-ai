# Gumroad Cover Image Brief

## Product
Newsletter → Social AI

## Dimensions
1280 × 720px (Gumroad standard) — also works as Twitter/X card

## Visual Concept

**Theme:** Dark, premium, minimal. Not a screenshot, not stock art.

**Background:** Very dark (#0A0A0A or near-black) — matches the app's UI

**Layout — left side (60%):**
- Large headline text (white, bold):
  ```
  Newsletter → Social
  ```
- Subline (muted indigo/purple, smaller):
  ```
  Paste your newsletter.
  Get a week of posts.
  ```
- Bottom left: "Built by BuildProven" in small muted text

**Layout — right side (40%):**
A stylized UI preview — not a full screenshot, more of an abstract render:
- Dark card with indigo accent line at top
- 3 small "LinkedIn" pill labels stacked
- A single blurred line of post text beneath each
- A 🟢 High Reach badge on the top card
- Slight glow effect from the indigo accent (#6366F1)

**Typography:**
- Font: Inter or SF Pro Display (system font, bold)
- Headline: ~64-72px, white
- Subline: ~28px, indigo (#A5B4FC)

**Color palette:**
- Background: #0A0A0A
- Cards: #181818
- Accent: #6366F1 (indigo)
- Text: #F1F1F1 / #A5B4FC
- Success green for badge: #4ade80

**Do NOT include:**
- Generic stock photos
- Screenshots of the actual UI (too small to read)
- Cartoon illustrations
- Gradients that look like a 2018 SaaS landing page
- The word "AI" in giant letters

**Do include:**
- A subtle indigo glow effect (like the button glow in the app)
- The → arrow in the product name — it's the key symbol
- The clean, dark, "this is real software" aesthetic

## Alt concept (simpler):
Just dark background, the large headline "Newsletter → Social" in gradient text, and the subline. No UI chrome at all. Clean and confident.

## File format
PNG, 1280 × 720px, high DPI if possible (2x = 2560 × 1440px exported)
